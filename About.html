About

A young DJ out of Boise, "The Red Scare" is a persona of DJ/producer Andrew Yoder. A DJ of many genres under the EDM spectrum ranging from House, Trap, Dubstep and more. He has been influenced by many artists including Knife Party, Skrillex, Diplo, and Dillon Francis. After DJ-ing for 4 years now he has crafted a unique, non-traditional method of mixing unlike any other DJ, keeping a smooth flow taking audiences through an adventure of electronic music. Growing up in Idaho, he was exposed to music that ranged from AC/DC to Eminem. However, after discovering electronic music he knew he had found something special. His music career began with a simple iPod DJ app, which lead to his first turntable and the realization that he wanted take up a career in the music industry.Soon after, he started producing his own music. He upgraded to FL Studios and since then has released 2 EPs and numerous singles on Sound cloud.
